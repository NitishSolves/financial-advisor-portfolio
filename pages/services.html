Insurance & Protection Planning

Safeguarding your family's financial future with LIC's most trusted protection products

Life Insurance

Comprehensive life cover that ensures your family's financial stability and lifestyle continuity in your absence. Suitable for salaried individuals, business owners, and self-employed professionals.

Term Insurance Plans

High-coverage, low-premium pure protection plans providing a large sum assured to your family at the most affordable premium. Ideal for income earners at every stage of life.

Child Plans

Dedicated education and marriage planning policies that secure your child's future milestones regardless of life's uncertainties. Includes waiver of premium on parent's absence.

Endowment Plans

Dual-benefit policies offering both protection and savings — providing a guaranteed lump sum at maturity along with life cover throughout the policy term.

Money Back Plans

Periodic survival benefit payouts during the policy term, combined with full sum assured on maturity. Perfect for families requiring regular liquidity with insurance protection.

Tax Saving Plans (80C)

LIC policies offering income tax deductions under Section 80C and 10(10D), helping you save tax while building a protected financial corpus for your future.

Claim Support & Financial Assistance

Personal, end-to-end guidance through every step of the insurance claim process

Claim Documentation Help

I personally guide families through every required document — death certificate, medical records, policy bond, nominee ID proofs — ensuring nothing is missed that could delay settlement.

Claim Form Submission

Accurate, complete submission of claim forms to LIC branch office with all supporting documents, minimizing rejections and delays caused by incomplete paperwork.

LIC Office Follow-Up

Regular, persistent follow-ups with LIC branch officers and the regional office until your claim is fully processed — so you never have to chase them alone.

Disputed Claim Support

Guidance and advocacy when a claim is questioned or initially rejected — helping families understand LIC's requirements and providing the right documentation to resolve disputes.

Maturity Claim Assistance

Smooth processing of policy maturity claims — helping policyholders collect their entitled maturity proceeds, survival benefits, and bonuses without complications.

Urgent Claim Response

For time-sensitive claim situations — particularly death claims for families in immediate financial need — I provide priority support with same-day response and action.
